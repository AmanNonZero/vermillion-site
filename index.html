Our Story

Born from a reverence
for celebration

The Vermillion Studio was founded on a belief that an Indian wedding is not merely an event — it's a sacred tapestry of emotions, rituals, and generational joy that deserves to be woven with extraordinary care.

From intimate Anand Karaj ceremonies in Amritsar to grand Sangeet celebrations on the shores of Udaipur, we bring together India's finest artisans, decorators, chefs, and performers to create experiences that transcend expectation.

Our approach blends deep cultural understanding with contemporary sophistication — honouring every ritual while introducing moments of modern surprise and delight.

RP
Riya Patel
Founder & Creative Director
