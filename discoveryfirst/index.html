What if our wedding is under ₹15 lakh? +

Discovery works for weddings from ₹10L to ₹5Cr+. The deliverables scale to your budget. For smaller weddings, the value is even more concentrated — ₹1.5L of planning clarity on a ₹15L wedding prevents disproportionately expensive mistakes.

Do we need both families involved? +

Ideally, yes — at least for the Week 1 sessions. The biggest source of wedding stress isn't vendors. It's misaligned expectations between families. We structure the sessions to surface and resolve those early, before they become mid-planning crises.

How is this different from a "free consultation" every planner offers? +

A free consultation is a sales call. Discovery is a paid, structured engagement with concrete deliverables — a DNA brief, vision book, BOQ, and roadmap. You're paying for objectivity, market intelligence, and a framework. No upselling, no kickback incentives, no hidden agenda.

Absolutely. Think of Discovery as a second opinion — an independent audit of your plan, budget, and vendor lineup. Several of our clients have used the BOQ to renegotiate existing vendor contracts and saved multiples of the Discovery fee.

Can we do this remotely? +

Yes. We run Discovery over video calls for couples based outside Mumbai, Delhi, and Jaipur. The deliverables are identical. For destination weddings, we include a virtual venue and vendor landscape assessment for your target location.
